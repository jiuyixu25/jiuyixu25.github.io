About

Jiuyi (Joey) Xu is a first-year Ph.D. student in Robotics at the Colorado School of Mines. His research interests include, but are not limited to, generative AI (e.g., image generation, generative reinforcement learning). He holds an M.S. in Computer Science from the University of Southern California and a B.E. in Software Engineering from Dalian University of Technology. Previously, he was a student research assistant at USC’s Institute for Creative Technologies, working on open-vocabulary object detection (OVOD) and open-vocabulary semantic segmentation (OVSS). Beyond research, Joey is an active peer reviewer for the Journal of Computing in Civil Engineering and has contributed to multiple academic conferences. He is also a student member of IEEE, ACM, and ASCE.
